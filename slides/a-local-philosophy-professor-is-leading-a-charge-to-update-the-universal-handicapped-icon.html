A local philosophy professor is leading a charge to update the universal handicapped icon.

#learnsomethingneweveryday — May 21, 2013
Head Position
Head is forward to indicate forward motion.
Arm Angle
Arm is pointing backward to suggest dynamic mobility.
Wheel Cutouts
The symbol presents the wheel as being in motion.
Limbs
consistent with other body representations found in the ISO 7001 - DOT Pictograms
Leg Position
The leg has been moved forward to allow for more space between it and the wheel which allows for better readability and cleaner application of icon as a stencil.
